Steps to record the meeting and download meeting recording video on your PC.
    Steps to record the meeting
  1. To Record the meeting click on Start Recording button, it will show a pop up of Share your screen as shown in below screen.
  2. On click of Share button of share your screen pop up, your recording gets started and it shows the recording timer over the RecordRTC icon as shown in below screen.
  3. To stop the recording click on Stop Recording button.
    Steps to download the meeting recording video.
  1. Click on RecordRTC icon and click See All Recordings as shown in the below screen.
  2. On click of See All Recordings, below screen gets opened. Here you can see your latest recorded video (Meeting recording) file.
  3. To view the list of all the meeting recordings, click on Menu icon. The list as shown in the below screen, shows all the meeting recording files that have been recorded after adding the RecordRTC chrome plugin. The name of the meeting recording file contains the date of recording.
  4. To Edit the name of the meeting recording file, click on edit icon, it will show a pop up as shown in the below screen. In the pop up, edit the name of the file and click on OK button, your file name gets edited.
  5. To Delete the recording, click on delete icon. On click of delete icon, it will show a pop up as shown in the below screen. Click on OK button of the pop up to delete the recording permanently.
  6. To download the recording, click on More icon and click on Download of the recorded video as shown in the below screen OR click on the link here of "click here to download".
  7. You can upload the recording by clicking on upload icon. On click of upload button, it will provide option as shown in below screen.
    1. Upload to YouTube: You can upload the recorded file on YouTube.
